Harsha is a street photographer based out of Los Angeles. His photography predominantly focuses on the raw beauty of street scenes, embracing elements of minimalism, chaos, and striking silhouettes. His unique perspective has earned him widespread acclaim, landing him a coveted spot in prestigious LA art shows and exhibitions across numerous galleries in the city. Aside from photography he is a Cook and a Software Engineer.

Continuing his exploration of streets, Harsha remains steadfast in his commitment to capturing the soul of street photography, one frame at a time
